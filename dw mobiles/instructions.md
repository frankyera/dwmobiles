# Website Specification: Mobile, Accessories & Gift Hub

## 1. Project Overview
A professional showcase website for a business specializing in mobile phones, accessories, and gift items. The primary goal is to display products, build brand awareness, and drive foot traffic to the physical store.

**Note:** No online transactions or payment gateways required.

---

## 2. Sitemap (Structure)
* **Home:** Hero banner, featured products, and brief "About Us".
* **Product Categories:** * Mobile Phones
    * Mobile Accessories (Chargers, Covers, etc.)
    * Gift Items
* **Gallery:** High-quality photos of the shop interior and new arrivals.
* **Contact Us:** Google Maps integration, contact form, and social media links.

---

## 3. Key Features & Functionality

### A. Dynamic Product Showcase
* A "Catalog" view where users can browse items.
* Filter/Category options (e.g., Filter by brand: Samsung, Apple).
* Product Detail Page: Image, Description, and a "Inquire via WhatsApp" button.

### B. Customer Engagement Tools
* **WhatsApp Integration:** A floating button for instant chat.
* **Click-to-Call:** Quick dial buttons for mobile users.
* **Newsletter Signup:** Simple email collection for "New Stock" alerts.

### C. Design & UI/UX
* **Mobile Responsive:** Must work perfectly on smartphones.
* **Modern Aesthetic:** Clean, tech-focused design with high-quality imagery.
* **Fast Loading:** Optimized images for quick browsing.

---

## 4. Technical Requirements
* **Platform:** WordPress (Recommended for easy updates) or Static HTML/Tailwind.
* **SEO:** Basic On-page SEO (Meta titles/descriptions for "Mobile shop in [City Name]").
* **Hosting:** Shared hosting with SSL certificate (HTTPS).

---

## 5. Content Provided by Owner
* High-resolution logos.
* Photographs of the shop and products.
* Business location (Google Map link).
* Contact details (Phone, Email, Social Media handles).
